Waffle Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 spoons of corn starch
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of melted unsalted butter
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of tea of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence

Instruction

  1. Sift the flour, sugar, starch, yeast and salt into a bowl. Reserve.
  2. In another container, mix the beaten eggs with the milk, the melted butter and the vanilla essence.
  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly incorporate the ingredients.
  4. Heat up the waffle iron. Place a shallow ladle of dough and spread until it covers the appliance mold, close the lid and let it bake until the dough is golden brown.
  5. Remove with the silicone spatula. Serve with honey, fruit or jam.

Additional Information

You can serve these waffle for breakfast with jam.
